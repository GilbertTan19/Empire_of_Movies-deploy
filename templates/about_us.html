Why Empire of Movies Exists

Empire of Movies' mission is to connect people who wants to discuss and give reviews of films they watched. We also provide complete database about a movie the first time they are requested in a matter of seconds and will be updated from time to time.


Aim

We hope to make the exchange of opinion about films to be accessible and user-friendly to everybody through a well-organized forum. Moreover, We hope that the users can easily access the updates by adding our bot on their telegram app.


Gathering around Movie and Discussion

Empire of Movies is all about Movie and Discussion as they are the heart of our website. Movies such as Film , TV-Series, as well as discussion about How a film can progress and continue to the second series, how the character develop throughout the movie, how an actor/ actress fits their character so much and Discussion about fan theories and unresolved mysteries in the movie. Empire of Movies is a place where you can search or request movies you are interested in and Discuss about your favourite movie with other people.
