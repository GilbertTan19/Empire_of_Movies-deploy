User Guide

Sign Up
  1. To sign up, fill in the form or log in with your Google or Facebook account
Login
  1. To log in, fill in the form or log in with your Google or Facebook account
Request Movie
  1. Enter the movie title and year (optional) if the movie isn’t available on the website yet
  2. Click on Submit
  3. The list of possible movies will appear
  4. Click on the movie you want to be added
  5. It will be available on the website (with information about the movie), and you can follow, create a discussion thread, or post  a review
All Movies
  1. This will display the list of available movies
Follow/Unfollow Movie
  1. Click on the follow button to follow a movie
  2. It will appear on the “Followed” page
  3. You will be notified when a new discussion thread or review is posted
  4. Click on the Unfollow button to unfollow a movie
Followed Movies
  1. This will display the list of movies you followed
Connect to Telegram

Connect to Telegram to be notified via a telegram bot when a new discussion thread or review of a movie you follow is posted

  1. Follow the instruction on the page
Discussion

Discussion page to discuss about the film

  1. To create a new discussion, you can either:
    • Option 1: Go to a movie page and click on "Discussion", and then click on the “New discussion” button to create a discussion thread, or
    • Option 2: Click on the on the navbar, and click on "New Discussion"
  2. You can edit or delete the discussion you created later
Discussion: Report, Comment, and Upvote/Downvote
  1. Open a discussion detail page
  2. If you find that a particular discussion is a spam or inappropriate, please report it by pressing the button indicated by (1) and click the “Report” button (2), and a prompt will appear for you to state the reason
  3. You will be able to interact with other users via the comment section on the discussion thread by typing it on (3) and submit it
  4. You can upvote or downvote a discussion thread (4) if you find it interesting to make it appear on top of the page if you sort it by “Top”
Review

You can give a review about a film

  1. To create a new review, you can either:
    • Option 1: Go to a movie page and click on "Review", and then click on the “New Review” button to create a review, or
    • Option 2: Click on the on the navbar, and click on "New Review"
  2. You can edit or delete the review you created later
  3. If you find that a particular review is a spam or inappropriate, please report it by pressing the button indicated by (1) and click the “Report” button (2), and a prompt will appear for you to state the reason
Web Notification
  1. If there is a notification, the notification button (1) will turn red
  2. To open the notifications list, click on the notification icon (1)
  3. To clear a notification, click on (3)
  4. To clear all notifications, click on (2)
  5. If there is no notification, the notification button (1) will turn white
Telegram Notification
  1. You must have previously followed the instruction on the “Connect to Telegram” page (have sent the command similar to what is indicated by (1). However, please follow the instruction from the page as the command may differ)
  2. If a movie you followed has a new discussion or review, you will be notified via our Telegram bot as indicated by (2)
Search Bar and Result
  1. You can use the search bar with “filter by” feature
  2. Choose the filters (filter by), enter the search query, and hit Enter on your keyboard
  3. The search result will appear
