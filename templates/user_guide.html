Sign Up

Login

Request movie

  • Enter the movie title and year (optional) if the movie isn’t available on the website yet
  • Click on Submit
  • The list of possible movies will appear
  • Click on the movie you want to be added
  • It will be available on the website (with information about the movie), and you can follow, create a discussion thread, or post  a review

Follow movie

  1. Click on the follow button to follow a movie
  2. It will appear on the “Followed” page
  3. You will be notified when a new discussion thread or review is posted
  4. Click on the Unfollow button to unfollow a movie

Connect to Telegram 

Connect to Telegram to be notified via a telegram bot when a new discussion thread or review of a movie you follow is posted

  1. Follow the instruction on the web

Discussion

Discussion page to discuss about the film

  1. To create a new discussion, you can either:
    • Click on the on the navbar, and click on "New Discussion" or
    • Go to a movie page and click on "Discussion", and then click on the “New discussion” button to create a discussion thread. You can edit or delete the discussion you created later
  2. You can upvote or downvote a discussion thread if you find it interesting to make it appear on top of the page if you sort it by “Top”

Report & Comment

  1. If you find that a particular discussion is a spam or inappropriate, please report it by pressing the “Report” button, and a prompt will appear for you to state the reason
  2. You will be able to interact with other users via the comment section on the discussion thread

Review

You can give a review about a film

  1. You can see all reviews about the film or create a new one
  2. To create a new review, you can either:
    • Click on the on the navbar, and click on "New Review" or
    • Go to a movie page and click on "Review", and then click on the “New discussion” button to create a discussion thread. You can edit or delete the discussion you created later
  3. You can fill in your review

Web notification

  1. If there is new notification, the notification button will turn red and if you clear all of the notifications, it will be white-colored

Telegram notification

You must have previously followed the instruction on the “Connect to Telegram” page

If a movie you followed has a new discussion or review, you will be notified via a telegram bot
